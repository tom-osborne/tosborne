Hi, I’m Tom — an Epicor ERP consultant with a background in manufacturing systems, custom development, and business process automation. I work closely with companies to streamline workflows, build reliable Epicor solutions, and bridge the gap between technical systems and day-to-day operations.


## This site

This site started as a tool to help me document my learnings and notes. It also provided a playground for me to indulge in my hobby of programming.

It's built with [Astro](https://astro.build) and [Chirping Astro](https://github.com/kannansuresh/chirping-astro), which I have really enjoyed using.
