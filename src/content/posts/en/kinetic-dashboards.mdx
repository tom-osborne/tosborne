I always recomment starting from scratch when building a dashboard. The conversion tool can do a decent job, but it will often cause a lot of confusion when you need to make changes. I've found that, while it may take a little longer to build from scratch, you will end up knowing the Dashboard far better and it will make future maintenance much easier.

# Creating the Dashboard
The initial setup of a Dashboard is quite straighforward. The first thing to do is to add the DataViews that we need, and to do this, I would recommend making use of the Wizard.

<Callout type="info">
  DataViews are the foundation of a Kinetic App.
</Callout>
